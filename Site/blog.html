    About the Blog

    I’ve spent the past year developing my craft as a trader through the principles of ICT (Inner Circle Trader). My focus lies in analyzing liquidity, market structure, and the psychology behind price movement. Each trading session is an opportunity to refine my process, adapt to changing conditions, and strengthen my understanding of how institutional players shape the market. My approach centers on patience, precision, and continuous improvement — with the goal of maintaining consistency, discipline, and helping others see price action through a smart-money perspective.
